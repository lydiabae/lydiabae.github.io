Google | Phone support remarketing ads

Google offers a fantastic phone support service for new advertisers, providing dedicated AdWords experts to help set up first time campaigns for free. For users who find signing up online a little tricky, phone support is a huge win and leads to uplifts in conversion rates. To support this, I created a friendly animated banner advertising the service to users who had read about AdWords online, but failed to get up and running on their own.
