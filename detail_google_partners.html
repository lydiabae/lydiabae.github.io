Google Partners

Google Partners is a fantastic service for SMBs to onboard a trusted agency partner. The initiative works to provide a business with the support they need to grow their business online through Google products. To advertise these benefits, I created an animated banner to highlight the key benefits of becoming a Google Partner, encouraging agencies to register for certification.

Personalising the existing banner | Iconography

The core of Google’s identity is the intent to simplify and unify. The design has been streamlined to focus on the fundamentals, stripping back the excess to minimise and modernize.They are simple, modern, friendly, and sometimes quirky. Product icons are highly simplified, exaggerated and caricatured in nature so that they are appropriate for use at small sizes.

Motion provides meaning

Objects are presented to the user without breaking the continuity of experience even as they transform and reorganize. Motion is meaningful and appropriate, serving to focus attention and maintain continuity. Feedback is subtle yet clear. Transitions are efﬁcient yet coherent.

Light Blue, Light Grey and White

Google Partners logo lockups should be perfectly visible and must not merge with the background. Four standard background colours were created to ensure excellent colour contrast. Whenever possible, the Google Partner badge should be used on neutral background colours.
