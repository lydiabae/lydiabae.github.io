Illustration Style and Principles

The base is geometric and flat, with an introduction of texture and organic shapes adding sophistication and dimension. Quantum illustrations represent concepts and embraces abstraction, veering away from skeuomorphic photorealism. There is vivid color in unexpected places.

Color in Quantum Paper is inspired by bold color statements juxtaposed with muted environments, taking cues from contemporary architecture, road signs, pavement marking tape and sports courts. Emphasize bold shadows and highlights.
