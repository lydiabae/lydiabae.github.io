Google Compare

Google Compare provides users with the opportunity to review products in an organised and useful fashion making a potentially complex decision a little easier. Google wants to create a new product icon that will be used on Google Compare Favicon, App, Chrome Extension Icon, Landing Page, Email creatives, Team Swag and so on.

The new icon should communicate the core idea and intend of Google Compare product. The icon need to be simple, modern, friendly, and sometimes quirky.

Design approach

Product icon design is inspired by the tactile and physical quality of material. The icon is cut, folded, and lit as paper would be, but represented by simple graphic elements. The quality of the material is sturdy, with clean folds and crisp edges. The matte-like finish interacts with light through subtle highlights and consistent shadows.

Product icon grid | The product icon grid has been developed to facilitate consistency and establish a clear set of rules for the positioning of graphic elements. This standardization results in a flexible but coherent system.

Colour | Color is inspired by bold color statements juxtaposed with muted environments, taking cues from contemporary architecture, road signs, pavement marking tape, and sports courts. Emphasize bold shadows and highlights.

Shadow | Shadows create a sense of depth and are a key element in brining more personality and visual depth to the product icons. Ideally, all product icons should have one light source 45 degree angles and between 1 and 3 shadows.
