Hi, I’m Lydia Bae. I’m a freelance digital designer based in London who enjoys creating innovative websites and applications.

I am an award-winning designer who is passionate about creating meaningful and beautiful web experiences. I design digital solutions that tell a story, solve a need, educate, activate, and inspire. My intent with my designs is to take care of the user journey, help users achieve their goals, and ultimately drive business success. I specialise in UI design/Information Architecture and have worked alongside a number of international clients including Google and AXA.
