Pfizer UK - Fragmin

Fragmin [dalteparin sodium] is administered as a sub-cutaneous injection used to treat and prevent venous thromboembolism (blood clot) , and was launched in May 1990 as the first Low Molecular Weight Heparin LMWH+ by Pharmacia. They are now used in a wide number of indications, creating the need to appeal to a wide audience, including surgeons, clinicians, pharmacists, nurses and budget holders.

The site needs to: Appeal and target a wide variety of HCPs, Have a simple user journey – regardless of which audience/customer/HCP you are, Offer the resources and info previously supplied by the F2F meetings with hospital reps, Needs to be engaging – and offer a refreshed feel that gives relevant Fragmin info and makes the user want to visit/return to the site.

Considerations

I believe that in order for this website to meet client needs of a more consumer look and feel – away from the standard pharma website – it should be modern, interactive and intuitive.
